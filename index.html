Hi there! I'm an undergraduate at BITS Pilani, Goa Campus where I'm majoring in Physics and Computer Science. I am primarily interested in using artificial intelligence to develop methods in quantum and high-energy physics.

Currently, I'm working with Dr. Prasanta Kumar Das on learning quantum potentials using neural networks and with Dr. Raghunath Ratabole on formulating effective field theories in the Daubechies wavelet basis.

I previously interned at the Quantum Computing group at TU Munich with Irene López Gutiérrez to implement novel optimization strategies for neural network quantum ansatzes. At BITS, I was guided by Dr. Kinjal Banerjee on using neural network equations of state for constraining higher-order neutron star nuclear matter parameters. I was briefly also a student researcher at the APP Centre for Artificial Intelligence Research guided by Dr. Tirtharaj Dash where we collaborated with Dr. Lovekesh Vig at TCS Research and Innovation Labs on learning hierarchical representations of images.
